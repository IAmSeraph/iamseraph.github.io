SERAPH

Mark Rienstra

Hi, my name is Mark Rienstra, but online I'm known as Seraph/IAmSeraph. I was born on 24th of December 1996 and raised in the south of Limburg, in a small town near Maastricht.

For secondary education I went across the border to Belgium to study electrical engineering, after I finished that study I decided to study Game Engineering at Saxion Enschede, and later Applied Computer Science.

But neither of these studies peeked my interests, they didn't offer me any challenge. For this reason I don't have any certificates for my skills, but I like to think work shows more than paper.
